07 — mindset
Team ke liye
golden rules

Rejection personal mat lena

Woh services ko nahi bol rahe — ek stranger ko bol rahe hain. Free reel ke baad rejection almost zero ho jaati hai.

Kaam pehle, pitch baad mein

Jitna zyada free value doge, utna zyada trust banega. Trust ke baad close karna easy hai.

₹999 > ₹0 — Always

Seedha ₹9,999 maangna mushkil hai. ₹999 ek foot in the door hai. Phir upsell karna 10x easy ho jaata hai.

Follow up — sirf ek baar

Agar reply nahi aaya, ek follow up 3 din baad. Uske baad next cafe. Pushy mat bano — helpful bano.

Portfolio build hota hai saath mein

Har free reel ek portfolio piece hai. Jitna zyada kaam dikhega, utne zyada inbound leads aayenge baad mein.

Consistency beats intensity

5 cafes har hafta, consistently. Ek din mein 20 karne ki zarurat nahi. System follow karo, results aayenge.
